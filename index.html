Leonard Nimoy

as Spock

1931 - 2005

Leonard Nimoy was an actor, author , singer and photographer, his most well known roll was that of Spock in the Star Trek franchise. The character is a half-vulcan half-human star fleet officer. Together with James Kirk and the crew of the USS Enterprise, they explore the far reach of space. Leonard portrayed Spock from 1964, in Star Trek: The Original Serise to 2013 in the recent reboot film, Star Trek Into Darkness.
